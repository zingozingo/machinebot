Before we dive into machine learning, let's understand what we're measuring:

Sepals

The green, leaf-like parts that protect the flower bud before it opens.

  • Sepal Length: Measured from base to tip
  • Sepal Width: Measured at the widest point

Petals

The colorful parts that attract pollinators like bees and butterflies.

  • Petal Length: Measured from base to tip
  • Petal Width: Measured at the widest point

  • The model learns decision rules to classify new examples
  • Different features (measurements) have different importance
  • Simple models can achieve high accuracy on well-separated data
